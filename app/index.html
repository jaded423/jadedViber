JadedViber

JadedViber is a suite of personal automation tools that connect to your own Google Workspace account and carry out actions you ask them to perform — like drafting an email, finding a file, or updating a spreadsheet — without the manual clicking.

Operated by Joshua Brown · jaded423@gmail.com

What JadedViber does

JadedViber is a set of personal, self-hosted automation tools and Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers — including gsuite, leadscout, and n8n workflows. Once you authorize it, JadedViber acts on your own Google account to perform the specific tasks you request, so routine Workspace work can be driven from a single assistant interface instead of by hand.

Why it needs Google access

To do the work you ask for, JadedViber requests Google API access to the parts of your Workspace the task touches. It acts only on the account you authenticate, and only to complete the action you initiate:

Gmail

Search, read, draft, label, and send messages on your request.

Drive

List, read, create, move, and organize your files and folders.

Docs

Read and edit your documents.

Sheets

Read and write your spreadsheets.

Slides

Read and edit your presentations.

Calendar

Read and manage your events.

Tasks

Read and manage your task lists.

How your data is handled

JadedViber uses Google user data only to provide the feature you invoked. Your data is processed transiently to complete each request. It is not sold, not used for advertising, and not used to train AI/ML models.

Google API Services — Limited Use. JadedViber’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

See the full Privacy Policy for details.

You stay in control

You authorize JadedViber yourself, and you can revoke its access at any time from your Google Account permissions page. Revoking access immediately stops JadedViber from acting on your account.
